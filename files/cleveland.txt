Cleveland Orenthal Brown is a character from the animated television series Family Guy, and its spin-off series The Cleveland Show.[1][2] He is voiced by Mike Henry. In the first seven seasons of Family Guy, Cleveland is a frequently recurring character. As one of Peter Griffin's neighbors and friends, Cleveland is also one of the few recurring African American characters on the show. He was conceived during the seventh-inning stretch of a Cleveland Indians game.[3] His established profession was that of a deli owner.
